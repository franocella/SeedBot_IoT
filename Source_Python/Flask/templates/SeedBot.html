Smart Farming System

SeedBot is an intelligent farming system designed to enhance productivity and sustainability in agriculture. By using advanced sensors and IoT technologies, SeedBot provides precise control over sowing and accurate measurement of environmental parameters. Thanks to AI and machine learning, SeedBot can automatically sow the most suitable type of crop, optimizing growth and maximizing yields.
